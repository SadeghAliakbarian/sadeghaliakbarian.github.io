Sadegh Aliakbarian

 

I'm a PhD student at the Australian National University, working under supervision of Dr. Lars Petersson (ANU/CSIRO), Dr. Mathieu Salzmann (EPFL), and Dr. Basura Fernando (ACRV/A-STAR) and have pieces of advice from Prof. Stephen Gould (ANU/ACRV).
I am now with Five AI (Oxford Research Group) as a research intern, working on adversarial example and model robustness to such examples. Prior to that, I was at Qualcomm AI Research in Amsterdam as a research intern, focusing on deep generative models to learn stochasticity in human motion.
My research interests are machine learning and computer vision, with the focus on generative models.
